batch

added in 0.5.0

(batch p n delay f)

Evaluate function f for side effects with sequences of values taken from port p. An evaluation occurs when n items are taken from the port or at least one item has been taken from the port and at least delay milliseconds have elapsed since batch was evaluated or f was previously evaluated. Returns a port on which the total number of items processed will be available when the port passed to batch is closed.
